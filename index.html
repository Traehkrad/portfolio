Yusuke Shinoko (死野子 遊助)

Concept Artist, Storyteller, & Game Designer

Who am I?

A Chinese American male who is interested in drawing and storytelling. My main medium for telling stories would be traditional manga and physical and digital video games. My main art style is traditional art with pencil, paper, pen, and eraser in manga style.

I know a bit of coding involving Python, HTML, and C++, but I am one hundred percent solely interested in creating manga and stories, not websites or coding.

Stories to Tell

My inspirations for my stories can originate from a multitude of things, from the common and mundane objects to the extraordinary. I hope that through my stories that other people could be inspired and learn certain philosophical lessons from them.

My favorite stories come from manga, comics, movies, and novels and my preferred genres are Fantasy and Science Fiction.

My Team Role

Outside of art, I have had experience in teaching, cooperation, and team leading. In high school, I volunteered as a tutor there which helped me with interacting with a variety of people.

Later, I would begin my own Manga Drawing Club near the end of my high school years and learned the difficulty of managing a team larger than the amount of people in my class projects.

In my early college years, I attempted to recruit students into my game design team and began on a project that didn't get very far. I learned the difficult way about communication and tailoring the skill set and experience of my members after we fell out.
